Evan Skinner

Alignment

Idaho
idaho.gov

Alignment helps to organize information to make it clearer and more professional looking. Idaho's official government website is a great example of alignment and proximity. From the home page most everything is kept centered with the objects above and below.

Proximity

Pinterest
pinterest.com


Proximity helps to establish relationships between items. Pinterest can be a very crowded site but for the most part it is a good example of keeping space between objects as not to interfere and distract from each other.

Contrast

Hewlett Packard
hp.com


Contrast can be determined by the difference in color and brightness of pictures and text on a website. HP does a very good job of keeping the contrast pleasing to the eye and maintaining simplicity in their design.
